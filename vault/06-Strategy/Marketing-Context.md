# Product Marketing Context

> Source: `.agent/product-marketing-context.md` — last updated 2026-03-05

## One-liner
Institutional-grade real estate advisory for global capital in Dubai.

## Business Model
Performance-based advisory (RERA Licensed) + standard brokerage fees.
"The One-Price Advantage" — no hidden costs.

## Target Audience
High-net-worth global expats, seasoned RE investors, tech/finance professionals seeking tax-free yields.
Sweet spot: AED 80K–400K annual rental budget / AED 1M–5M+ purchase budget.

## Personas
| Persona | Cares about | Our promise |
|---|---|---|
| The Savvy Expat | Tax efficiency & liquidity | Objective, data-backed selection |
| The Global Yield Hunter | Net ROI & capital preservation | Institutional stress-testing of assets |
| The Currency Hedger | USD-pegged stability | Hard-currency asset accumulation |

## Core Differentiators
1. **The Analytical Filter** — strict internal stress tests, pass/fail verdicts
2. **Institutional Scale** — pre-launch + off-market inventory via strategic alliances
3. **The North Capital Verdict** — contrarian advice including "The Bear Case" (will tell you when to pass)

## Why Competitors Fall Short
- Standard brokers: volume-focused, not strategy-focused
- Wealth managers: lack localized Dubai RE expertise
- PropTech apps: data without institutional interpretation

## Messaging Pillars
- Tax-free (0% income tax, 0% CGT)
- USD-pegged (AED = currency hedge)
- Data-driven (DLD-backed, not marketing fluff)
- Contrarian (we say "no" when others say "buy")
