# Growth Strategy — North Capital DXB

> Source: `.agent/STRATEGY.md` — last updated 2026-03-08
> Model: Content-Led Authority → reputation-led advisory

## Core Problem
Trust is the purchase trigger for HNW advisory.
Funnel: **Awareness → Authority → Trust → Booking**
Currently: almost nothing at the top.

## Target
2-3 qualified calls/week within 90 days. 1-2 clients/month within 6 months.
Revenue: high-ticket advisory (not PLG, not paid ads yet).

## 90-Day Scorecard
| Metric | Now | 30d | 60d | 90d |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| X followers | ~0 | 100 | 250 | 500+ |
| LinkedIn connections | ~0 | 150 | 300 | 500+ |
| Blog posts | ~0 | 8 | 16 | 25+ |
| Email subscribers | 0 | 50 | 150 | 300+ |
| Qualified calls/week | 0 | 1 | 2 | 3-4 |
| Clients closed | 0 | 1 | 2-3 | 4-6 |

## Phase 1 — Fix Distribution (0-30 days)
1. **Founder-forward X account** — make it feel like a person, not a brand bot
2. **First X thread** — shareable data insight (not promotional)
3. **LinkedIn personal profile** — personal > company page for advisory
4. **Activate fintech network** — 20-30 warm contacts, personal DMs

## Phase 2 — Audience Machine (30-90 days)
- X: 2x deal posts/day + 1x human commentary
- Blog SEO: 3 content pillars
  1. Dubai Market Intelligence (area deep-dives, supply/demand)
  2. Investor Mechanics (yield calc, ROI, tax efficiency)
  3. **The Bear Cases** — "don't buy this" content = highest trust, no competitor does this
- Email list: capture from blog (newsletter or gated PDF)
- Reddit/LinkedIn community infiltration (contribute data, don't pitch)

## Phase 3 — Compound (90-180 days)
- Borrowed audiences (Dubai expat podcasts, UAE finance newsletters)
- Alliance partner co-content
- Distress deals terminal as lead magnet (email gate for advanced filters)

## Key Insight: Bear Cases = Moat
No Dubai broker publishes genuine "don't buy this" content. This is the highest-trust content possible and our competitive moat. Every post that says "avoid this area" builds more trust than 10 "great opportunity" posts.

## What NOT to Do Right Now
- Paid advertising — no credibility base yet
- More features — distribution is the problem, not product
- LinkedIn company page focus — personal profile converts 3-5x better
